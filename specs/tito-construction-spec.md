## PART 2: NEW DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Design Philosophy
Bold, dark, premium aesthetic that commands respect and showcases craftsmanship. The design should feel like a high-end construction firm - confident, skilled, and trustworthy. Primary goal: Impress visitors with portfolio work and make requesting a quote effortless.

### Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| **Primary Dark** | Charcoal Black | #1A1A1A | Primary backgrounds, headers |
| **Secondary Dark** | Slate Gray | #2D2D2D | Cards, secondary backgrounds |
| **Accent** | Copper/Bronze | #B87333 | CTAs, highlights, accents |
| **Accent Light** | Warm Gold | #D4A84B | Hover states, icons |
| **Text Primary** | Off-White | #F5F5F0 | Headings, primary text |
| **Text Secondary** | Warm Gray | #A3A3A3 | Body text, captions |
| **Contrast** | Pure White | #FFFFFF | Buttons, key elements |

*Rationale: Dark palette conveys premium craftsmanship and sophistication. Copper/bronze accents evoke tools, hardware, and quality materials. Creates a bold, masculine aesthetic appropriate for construction.*

### Typography

| Element | Font | Weight | Size (Desktop) | Size (Mobile) |
|---------|------|--------|----------------|---------------|
| **H1 (Hero)** | Playfair Display | 700 | 64px | 40px |
| **H2 (Section)** | Playfair Display | 600 | 48px | 32px |
| **H3 (Card)** | Inter | 600 | 28px | 22px |
| **Body** | Inter | 400 | 18px | 16px |
| **Small/Caption** | Inter | 400 | 14px | 14px |
| **CTA Buttons** | Inter | 700 | 16px | 16px |
| **Nav** | Inter | 500 | 15px | 14px |

*Rationale: Playfair Display (serif) for headlines adds elegance and premium feel. Inter for body maintains readability on dark backgrounds.*
